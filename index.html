Why It Matters: Unlocking E-Waste Potential

Knowing what's inside your old electronics is the first step towards responsible disposal and maximizing resource recovery. Every discarded device contains valuable materials that can be reused, recycled, or sold, preventing environmental harm and contributing to a circular economy.

Recycling Saves Resources:

  • Recycling 1 million laptops saves energy equivalent to powering 3,500 homes for a year.
  • For every 1 million cell phones recycled, 35,000 lbs of copper, 772 lbs of silver, 75 lbs of gold, and 33 lbs of palladium can be recovered.
  • Manufacturing new electronics from recycled materials significantly reduces water and air pollution.
